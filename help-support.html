How do I add items to my pantry?
You can add items in two ways: tap the "+" button to add manually, or use the camera icon to scan a product barcode. The barcode scanner will automatically look up product information when available.
Can I share my pantry with family members?
Yes! Go to Settings and tap on your pantry to invite members. They'll receive an invitation to join, and once accepted, everyone can view and edit the shared pantry in real-time.
How do expiration reminders work?
When you add an expiration date to an item, Pantry will send you a notification before it expires. You can customize how many days in advance you want to be reminded in Settings > Notifications.
Is my data backed up?
Yes, all your pantry data is automatically synced to the cloud. If you sign in on a new device with the same account, all your pantries and items will be there.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently delete your account and all associated data. This action cannot be undone.
The barcode scanner isn't recognizing my product
Not all products are in our database. If a barcode isn't recognized, you can still add the item manually. Make sure you have good lighting and hold the camera steady over the barcode.
