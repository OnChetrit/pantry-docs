Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 19, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Pantry ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Summary: We collect only the data necessary to provide you with a great pantry management experience. Your data is stored securely and is never sold to third parties.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account using Apple Sign-In, we receive your name and email address (or a private relay email if you choose to hide it).
  • Pantry Data: Items you add to your pantry, including names, quantities, expiration dates, and categories.
  • Shopping Lists: Items you add to your shopping cart within the app.
  • Camera Access: When you use the barcode scanner feature, we access your camera to scan product barcodes. We do not store images or video from your camera.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Pantry app functionality
  • Sync your pantry data across your devices
  • Send expiration reminders and notifications (if enabled)
  • Improve and optimize the app experience

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication via Apple Sign-In
  • Row-level security policies to ensure you can only access your own data

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your pantry data may be shared only in the following circumstances:

  • Pantry Sharing: If you choose to share a pantry with family members or household members, they will be able to see and edit items in that shared pantry.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of notifications

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
